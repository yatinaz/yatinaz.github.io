IIT Kanpur · 2020–2022  ·  Supervisor: Prof. Nitin Gupta

TreadWill — Gamified CBT Portal for Depression

Designed and developed a web portal delivering Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) through gamified mental imagery exercises targeting cognitive distortions. Based on the established principle that depressed individuals interpret ambiguous situations negatively; the game trains users to reframe this bias. Led front-end engineering for cross-browser compatibility across Chrome, Firefox, Safari and mobile WebKit. Research published in JMIR Games (2023).
